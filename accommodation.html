Room Details & Amenities

Grace@C is a stylishly furnished apartment located on the cliffs of Herolds Bay in the Monate Eco Estate. This property boasts a beautiful sea view and is ideally located only 9 km from the George Airport.

This apartment comfortably sleeps 4 guests and comprises 2 bedrooms, 1 bathroom, a kitchen, and a living area. Both bedrooms have a queen-size bed and share use of the bathroom fitted with a bath, shower, toilet, and washbasin.

The open-plan kitchen downstairs is equipped with a fridge-freezer, microwave, cutlery and crockery, as well as tea- and coffee-making facilities. This area further extends into the open-plan living area that has a comfortable couch and a flat-screen TV.

Free Wi-Fi is also available. The sliding doors open onto the balcony where guests can enjoy the amazing ocean views and secure parking is available inside the Monate Eco Estate which has electric fencing and 24-hour CCTV cameras. Guests also have access to a hot tub.
